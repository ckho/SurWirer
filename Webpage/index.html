Characteristic

Simplicity

  • Easy to control through a user friendly and simple apps!
  • Even child can use it well

Accuracy

  • Detection with only +/- 2 bpm difference from standard PPG system. (Finger Mode)

Wearable

  • Light in weight!
  • Do not get hot even turning it on for a long time.
  • Do not have any exposed bottom, everyone can wear it comfortably.

Wireless

  • Bluetooth 4.0 Low Energy Data Transmission
  • Follow Heart Rate Profile Standard
  • Compatiable with any Apps/Receiver following same protocol
  • Allow multiple connection, which is useful in hospital situation. E.g. receiving signals from multiple SurWirer from different patients

Multiple Mode

  • Finger and Wrist can be selected
  • Extremely accurate at measuring finger
  • Less accurate at wrist
  • Need further improvement in accuracy for wrist measuring (many scientists and engineers are doing this research in the world now)

Power Saving

  • Our sensor is off automatically when disconnected
  • Low power ARM MCU is used.
  • Usage time up to two weeks, much longer than an Apple Watch

Mobile

  • Cross-platform - Cordova Framework is used.
  • Both iOS and Android with bluetooth 4.0 capacity is supported.
  • Integration with Apple HealthKit is Possible with Apple Developer Account.

Durable

  • High quality components and plastic bracket, device life time up to at least 5 years! (estimation under normal usage)

Further Development

  • Health advice based on the result (need to consult medical professional first)
  • Keep track of the record
  • Save the record together with Geolocation, Activity, Date & Time
  • Authorize family doctor to access the record and notify the doctor for abnormal value

Financial Concern
